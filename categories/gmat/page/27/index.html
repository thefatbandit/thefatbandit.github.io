6.5 Strengthening the Support for a Cause-and-Effect Conclusion

Lesson Content As we saw in the Strengthen the Argument chapter, there are many ways to strengthen an argument. Ruling out alternative causes. Showing that when the cause is not present the effect does not occur. Showing that the relationship cannot be reversed. 💡MUST KNOW: Three ways that are commonly used to strengthen the support for cause-and-effect conclusions are the following: 1) Ruling out alternative causes, 2) Showing that when the cause is not present the effect does not occur, 3) Showing that the relationship cannot be reversed.
