## Lesson Content
In the verbal section of the GMAT, between 9 and 13 of the 36 questions will be Sentence Correction questions.

In this course, we will cover all of the concepts, strategies, and skills that you’ll need to correctly answer every Sentence Correction question you see on test day.

> - Sentence Structure

> - Subject-Verb Agreement

> - Pronouns and Antecedents

> - Modifiers

> - Verb Tense, Mood, and Voice

> - Parallelism

> - Logical Comparisons

Let’s get started on mastering Sentence Correction by discussing the basic layout of Sentence Correction questions.
