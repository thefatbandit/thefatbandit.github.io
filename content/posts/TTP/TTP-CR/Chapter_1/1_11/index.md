## Lesson Content
Sometimes we find that we have eliminated all the answer choices.

{{< alert type="warning" >}}
💡<b>MUST KNOW:</b>

If you have eliminated all the answer choices, simply take a moment to be sure that you have properly understood the passage. Then go back through the answer choices and carefully reevaluate them. This time through you need to really bear down on each answer choice and rigorously evaluate its merits.
{{< /alert >}}

By continuing to consider the differences between the choices and by going back to the passage to find details that you missed in previous readings, you’ll find that one choice that is the correct answer to the question.

Let’s now discuss what we have to see about the statements in passages and answer choices to do well in Critical Reasoning.
