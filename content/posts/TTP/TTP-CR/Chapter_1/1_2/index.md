## Lesson Content
Each Critical Reasoning question begins with a written stimulus, which is usually, though not always, in the form of a short passage of about 100 words or fewer.

{{< alert type="warning" >}}
💡<b>MUST KNOW:</b>

Each Critical Reasoning question begins with a written stimulus, usually in the form of a short passage of about 100 words or fewer. Accompanying the stimulus is a question and five answer choices. The question will always ask us to determine which of the five answer choices is logically related to the stimulus in a particular way.
{{< /alert >}}

Of course, the correct answer to a Critical Reasoning question will be the choice that matches what the question asks us to find.

To see how a Critical Reasoning question works, let’s consider an example.

##  Example 1 

Recently, sales of figs have dramatically increased in many areas of the country.

Which of the following if true provides the most additional support for the conclusion of the argument?

1. [ ] (A) Because fig trees can thrive in a range of climates, it is possible to grow figs in many areas of the country.
    > <br> <b>Correct Answer:</b> (D)
2. [ ] (B) Sales of blueberries also have increased recently.
    > <br> <b>Correct Answer:</b> (D)
3. [ ] (C) Not only sales of fresh figs but also sales of dried figs have increased recently.
    > <br> <b>Correct Answer:</b> (D)
4. [x] (D) Sales of figs have increased only in areas of the country in which the new video game is popular.
5. [ ] (E) Figs are sources of important nutrients and of antioxidants.
    > <br> <b>Correct Answer:</b> (D)

{{< /quizdown >}}

   <h5><details><summary><b><u>View Solution</u></b></summary>
   {{< alert type="secondary" >}}

##### Question Level: easy



This question is a typical Critical Reasoning question.


It starts off with a passage, which, in this case, presents an argument about why sales of figs have increased.


The conclusion of the argument presented is the following:


<u>Conclusion</u>: The reason for the increases in sales of figs is people’s seeing figs portrayed as sources of power in the new video game.


The passage is followed by a question stem:


<u>Question Stem</u>: Which of the following, if true, provides the most additional support for the conclusion of the argument?”


So, to correctly answer the question, we need to find the choice that provides the most additional support for the argument’s conclusion.


> <b>(A) Because fig trees can thrive in a range of climates, it is possible to grow figs in many areas of the country.
</b>


 This choice does not provide support for the conclusion. It provides some information on growing figs but says nothing that indicates why fig sales increased or connects the increases in sales of figs to the release of the new video game.


> <b>(B) Sales of blueberries also have increased recently.
</b>


 The fact that sales of blueberries have increased does not support the conclusion at all. To support the conclusion, we need information that somehow indicates that the increases in sales of figs are related to the release of the new video game, and the fact that sales of blueberries increased does not indicate that the two are related.


> <b>(C) Not only sales of fresh figs but also sales of dried figs have increased recently.
</b>


 Notice that this choice provides additional information about the increases in sales of figs but does not provide more information on why the increases occurred. So, what it says does not indicate that the increases in sales of figs are related to the release of the new video game.


> <span class="correct-answer"><i class="fal fa-check-circle"></i><span class="ms-1 fw-semibold correctness-text">YOUR CHOICE</span></span><b>(D) Sales of figs have increased only in areas of the country in which the new video game is popular.
</b>


 This choice provides support for the conclusion that the reason for the increases in sales of figs is people’s seeing figs portrayed as sources of power in the new video game.


The passage supports the conclusion by providing the information that the increases in sales of figs occurred just after the video game was released.


This choice provides further support by showing that the increases in sales of figs occurred not only just after the video game was released but also only in areas in which the video game is popular. After all, if sales of figs increased only in areas in which the video game is popular, then we have reason to believe that there is a connection between the video game and the increases in sales of figs.


So, since this choice provides additional support for the argument’s conclusion, this choice is the correct answer.


> <b>(E) Figs are sources of important nutrients and of antioxidants.
</b>


 This choice has the wrong effect.


We need a choice that provides support for the conclusion that the reason for the increases in sales of figs is people’s seeing figs portrayed as sources of power, but this choice instead provides a different reason why people would purchase figs, the fact that figs are sources of nutrients and antioxidants.


> ##### Correct Answer: (D)
