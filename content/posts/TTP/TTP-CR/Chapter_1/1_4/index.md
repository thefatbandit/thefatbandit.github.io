## Lesson Content
Most GMAT Critical reasoning questions are based on arguments.

{{< alert type="warning" >}}
💡<b>MUST KNOW:</b>

An argument is a series of statements that logically explain a point of view.
{{< /alert >}}

In order to effectively answer Critical Reasoning questions, we must understand how arguments are commonly structured.

> - The Conclusion

> - The Evidence

> - The Assumptions

> - The Background Information

Let’s begin by discussing the conclusion.
