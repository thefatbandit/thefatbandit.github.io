## Lesson Content
As you already know, Complete the Passage questions are, for the most part, just altered forms of questions of types that you already know well.

Therefore, you already have a solid understanding of many of the common types of wrong answers that appear in Complete the Passage questions.

At the same time, since Complete the Passage questions have their own unique format, they also have some fairly unique types of incorrect answers.
