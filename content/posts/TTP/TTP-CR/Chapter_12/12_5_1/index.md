## Lesson Content
The question stems that appear in Boldface questions generally ask us to determine which answers accurately describe the bolded sections of the passages.

> <em>In the argument given, the two boldfaced portions play which of the following roles?</em>

> <em>In the argument above, the portion in boldface plays which of the following roles?</em>

> <em>In the ecologist's reasoning, the two portions in </em><em>boldface</em><em> play which of the following roles?</em>

> <em>Which of the following, if true, would serve the same function in the argument as the statement in boldface?</em>

{{< alert type="warning" >}}
💡<b>MUST KNOW:</b>

Be ready to identify what you have to do when you see a question stem referring to sections of a passage that are in boldface type.
{{< /alert >}}

Let’s now discuss what is probably the most important move you can make in handling Boldface questions, finding conclusions.
