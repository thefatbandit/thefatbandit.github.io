## Lesson Content
Many Critical Reasoning questions require that we determine what information is missing from the arguments presented to us.

{{< alert type="warning" >}}
💡<b>MUST KNOW:</b>

The ability to determine what information is missing from arguments is of significant importance to success in answering Critical Reasoning questions.
{{< /alert >}}

We’ll elaborate upon this point as the course proceeds, but we are about to see this important point front and center with assumptions.
