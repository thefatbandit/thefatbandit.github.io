## Lesson Content
The ways in which correct answers weaken arguments are predictable.

As we go through these answer types, there are, at least, three things that you can accomplish.

> • You can learn about the types of answers discussed, any of which could easily show up in Critical Reasoning questions on the GMAT.

> • You can note the logic that underlies each answer type, as that logic could show up in Weaken questions or in questions of other types.

> • You can note how logically and convincingly the correct answers to Weaken questions generally weaken the arguments.
