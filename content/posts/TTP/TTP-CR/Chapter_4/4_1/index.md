## Lesson Content
Strengthen the Argument questions are among the most common types of GMAT Critical Reasoning Questions.

The good news is that, as promised, the skills that you learned in the Assumption Chapter – and reinforced in the Weaken the Argument Chapter – will be significant assets to you as you learn to handle Strengthen the Argument questions.

Let’s begin discussing how to effectively strengthen an argument.
