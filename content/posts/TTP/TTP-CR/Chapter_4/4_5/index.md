## Lesson Content
While there are many ways in which an argument can be strengthened, the following five ways show up consistently in GMAT Strengthen the Argument questions:

> 1) Provide a Premise to Support or Add Support for the Conclusion

> 2) Explicitly State an Assumption upon which the Argument Depends

> 3) Address an Issue that Could Affect the Validity of the Conclusion

> 4) Rule out an Alternate Cause of an Effect

> 5) Confirm a Conclusion by Changing, Adding, or Eliminating a Variable

Let’s discuss each of these.
