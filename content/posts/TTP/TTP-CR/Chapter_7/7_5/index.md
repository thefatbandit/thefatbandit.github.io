## Lesson Content
While we know that there is only one correct answer to each Inference question, in most Inference questions, as we have already seen, some of the incorrect answers may somehow rather convincingly seem to be correct and, thus, may tempt test takers for a variety of reasons.

In order to mentally prepare you to handle these trap answers, and thus reduce the likelihood of your falling for them, let’s go over some of the types of trap answers that can appear in Inference questions.

Let’s begin by looking at answers that could be true or is likely to be true.
