## Lesson Content
In Evaluate the Argument questions, there are four forms in which answer choices can appear:

> - Questions that can be answered “Yes” or “No”

> - Questions that can be answered in two ways that are not “Yes” or “No”

> - Questions of other types

> - Statements, rather than questions

We have already discussed how to handle answer choices that can be answered “Yes” or “No.” We use the Yes/No test and consider whether the “Yes” and “No” answers strengthen or weaken the arguments.
