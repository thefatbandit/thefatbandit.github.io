## Lesson Content
We must eliminate answer choices because we see why they are incorrect, not because we can’t see why they are correct.

{{< alert type="warning" >}}
💡<b>MUST KNOW:</b>

In answering Sentence Correction questions, we must eliminate choices because we see why they are incorrect, not because we can’t see why they are correct.
{{< /alert >}}

This brings up an important point.

{{< alert type="warning" >}}
💡<b>MUST KNOW:</b>

As you move through the Target Test Prep Course, you must be able to articulate why each of the four incorrect answer choices is incorrect. Don’t move on from incorrect choices until you fully understand why each is incorrect.
{{< /alert >}}

Let’s now discuss what to do if we are stuck between two final answer choices.
