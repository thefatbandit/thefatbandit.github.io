## Lesson Content
As you proceed, it will be helpful to keep in mind the fact that the errors that appear in Sentence Correction questions are limited to a handful of major topics.

(1) Subject-Verb Agreement

(2) Pronouns and Antecedents

(3) Modifiers

(4) Verb Tense, Mood, and Voice

(5) Parallelism

(6) Logical Comparisons

(7) Sentence Structure

(8) Idioms

(9) Logic and Meaning

Notice that, at a high level, there are not too many different types of errors that appear.
