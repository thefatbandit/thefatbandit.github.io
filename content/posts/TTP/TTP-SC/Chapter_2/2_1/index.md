## Lesson Content
At the core of Sentence Correction is proper sentence structure, making this chapter one of the more important chapters in this course.

Although the information presented here is foundational, almost all students will find either that they never learned this material or that much of it has been long forgotten.

{{< alert type="warning" >}}
💡<b>MUST KNOW:</b>

Although the information presented here is foundational, almost all students will find either that they never learned this material or that much of it has been long forgotten.
{{< /alert >}}

In either case, without a mastery of the information presented here, you may find that most of the chapters that follow will be less than fully effective as learning tools.

So, take your time to learn this material well.

Let’s begin by discussing phrases and clauses.
