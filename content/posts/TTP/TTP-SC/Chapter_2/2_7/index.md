## Lesson Content
When talking about the structure of sentences, we divide sentences into four types:

> (1) Simple Sentences

> (2) Compound Sentences

> (3) Complex Sentences

> (4) Compound-Complex Sentences

We’ll carefully discuss each of these sentence types, beginning with the simple sentence.
