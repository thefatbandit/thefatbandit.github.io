## Lesson Content
A simple sentence consists of a single independent clause followed by a period.

{{< alert type="warning" >}}
💡<b>MUST KNOW:</b>

A simple sentence consists of a single independent clause followed by a period.
{{< /alert >}}

The following are examples of simple sentences:

> I do.

> You will.

> I was at the beach.

> John and Jeff were reading books.

> Spending time with animals is one of the best ways to enjoy your days.

We’ll discuss simple sentences in more detail as we progress, but for now it’s important to understand that the simple sentence is the simplest collection of words that can constitute an English sentence.
