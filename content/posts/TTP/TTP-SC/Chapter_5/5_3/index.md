## Lesson Content
As the name implies, a misplaced modifier is a modifier that is in the wrong place in a sentence.

{{< alert type="warning" >}}
💡<b>MUST KNOW:</b>

The misplacement of modifiers results in errors in meaning, and misplaced modifiers are frequently used in incorrect answer choices in Sentence Correction questions.
{{< /alert >}}

Misplaced modifier questions can be particularly challenging because even native English speakers often misplace modifiers in their speech and writing.

Let’s see how misplacement can affect the meaning of a sentence by considering the effects of misplacing adjectives.
