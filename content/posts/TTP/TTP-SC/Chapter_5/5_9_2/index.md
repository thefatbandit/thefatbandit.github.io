## Lesson Content
In Sentence Correction questions, participles often appear in participial phrases, which are phrases based on, and usually beginning with, participles.

Here are some examples of, present participial phrases (phrases based on present participles):

> spinning incessantly

> jumping in the grass

> successfully navigating the channel

The following phrases are past participial phrases (phrases based on past participles):

> ruined by vandals

> lost in the fog

> quickly forgotten by many

Let’s now discuss how participial phrases are used in sentences.
