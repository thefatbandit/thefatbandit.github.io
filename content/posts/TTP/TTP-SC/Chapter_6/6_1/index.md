## Lesson Content
Getting Sentence Correction questions correct often requires considering verb tense, mood, or voice.

However, as is the case with other aspects of sentence construction, too often people attempt to use their “ear” to answer questions involving these concepts.

In the following sections, we’ll cover various verb tense, mood, and voice related concepts and strategies.

Let’s begin by discussing the present tense.
