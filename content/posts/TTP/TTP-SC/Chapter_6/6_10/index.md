## Lesson Content
The continuous tenses, sometimes called “the progressive tenses,” are used in discussing events that are continuous.

{{< alert type="warning" >}}
💡<b>MUST KNOW:</b>

We use the progressive tenses to discuss actions that are, were, or will be in progress.
{{< /alert >}}

The purpose of these tenses is to bring the reader’s attention to the temporarily ongoing nature of an action.

{{< alert type="warning" >}}
💡<b>MUST KNOW:</b>

The purpose of the continuous tenses is to bring the reader’s attention to the temporarily ongoing nature of an action.
{{< /alert >}}
