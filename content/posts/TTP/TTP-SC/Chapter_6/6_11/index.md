## Lesson Content
Some Sentence Correction questions will test our skill in assessing use of verb mood.

{{< alert type="warning" >}}
💡<b>MUST KNOW:</b>

Whereas verb tense lets us place events in time, verb mood conveys the attitude with which a statement is made.
{{< /alert >}}

Let’s begin with the indicative verb mood.
