## Lesson Content
The indicative mood is the most commonly used verb mood and is used for stating facts, assertions, and observations.

> The flowers <strong>smell</strong> good.

> Tomorrow, we <strong>will go</strong> to the beach.

> It may <strong>be</strong> necessary to change the design of the outer shell.

> The panel <strong>concluded</strong> that the bank should <strong>stop</strong> financing oil projects completely by 2008.

> Once ranging as far east as Arkansas and Louisiana, ocelots <strong>are</strong> currently found only in extreme southern Texas and northeastern Mexico.
