## Lesson Content
People often wonder when to check for parallelism.

{{< alert type="warning" >}}
💡<b>MUST KNOW:</b>

There are firmly established triggers that alert us to check for proper parallelism. Each time we encounter one of these triggers in a sentence, we check to be sure that the elements in that sentence are parallel.
{{< /alert >}}

In this chapter, we’ll cover each of these parallelism triggers, such as the FANBOYS conjunctions, correlative conjunctions, including “not only … but also,” and items found in a list.

For now, it’s important that we know not to rely on our “ears” when checking for parallelism.
