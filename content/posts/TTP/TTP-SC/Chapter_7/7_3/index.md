## Lesson Content
A common theme we’ll encounter is that parallelism has little to do with how a sentence sounds.

We’ll encounter many sentences that “sound” parallel, yet are not.

{{< alert type="warning" >}}
💡<b>MUST KNOW:</b>

Parallelism has little to do with how a sentence sounds. Instead, parallelism has everything to do with how a sentence is structured. Don’t rely on your “ear”!
{{< /alert >}}

Let’s now discuss a concept that is foundational in understanding parallelism: structures built around coordinating conjunctions, also known as FANBOYS, must be parallel.
