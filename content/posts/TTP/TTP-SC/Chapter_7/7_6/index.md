## Lesson Content
A list consists of two or more items in a series, and the items in a list are generally separated by commas or coordinating conjunctions (FANBOYS).

The items in a list must be parallel.

{{< alert type="warning" >}}
💡<b>MUST KNOW:</b>

A list consists of two or more items in a series, often separated by commas. The items in a list must be parallel.
{{< /alert >}}

The good news is that, because you already know a lot about checking for parallelism across the FANBOYS conjunctions, the skills needed for checking for parallelism in a list will be much easier to master.

Now, to evaluate sentences with lists, we must understand what structures are understood to “carry over” in a structure involving a list.
