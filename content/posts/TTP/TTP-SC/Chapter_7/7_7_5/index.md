## Lesson Content
At this point, you’ve already learned a great deal about the correlative structure “not only … but also.” The good news, as promised, is that the patterns that apply to “not only … but also” apply almost identically to the other correlative conjunctions.

Let’s continue by talking about carry over and correlative conjunctions.
