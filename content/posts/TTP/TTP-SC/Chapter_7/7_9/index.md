## Lesson Content
Sometimes in learning about parallelism, people come away with the idea that the elements of a parallel structure must always match each other closely.

Let’s review some common situations in which elements that may not seem parallel are considered parallel, starting with the fact that verbs in different tenses can be perfectly parallel.
