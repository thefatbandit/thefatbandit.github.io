## Lesson Content
There are a number of signal words that can alert us that a comparison is being made.

> - in the same way

> - than

> - at the same rate

> - similar to

> - correspondingly

> - much as

> - so

> - whereas

> - while

> - nevertheless

> - nonetheless

> - by the same token

> - similarly

> - just as

> - likewise

> - in similar fashion

> - as … as

{{< alert type="warning" >}}
💡<b>MUST KNOW:</b>

Familiarize yourself with the common comparison signal words and be on the lookout for them. 
{{< /alert >}}

Let’s now discuss the important concept that comparisons must be logical.
