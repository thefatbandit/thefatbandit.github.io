## Lesson Content
Weaken the Argument questions are the most common type of Critical Reasoning questions on the GMAT.

The essence of a Weaken the Argument question is simple; it asks us to select an answer option that decreases the persuasiveness of an argument.

{{< alert type="warning" >}}
💡The essence of a Weaken the Argument question is simple; it asks us to select an answer option that decreases the persuasiveness of an argument. In other words, it asks us to weaken an argument.
{{< /alert >}}

The good news is that, via the previous chapter, you have mastered the idea of the assumption.

Let’s begin by discussing why it’s important to focus on the assumptions made by authors in Weaken the Argument questions.
