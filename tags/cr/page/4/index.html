3.5 Weaken Does Not Mean Destroy.

Lesson Content When we hear the phrase “weaken the argument,” we may feel that our job is to destroy the argument. In fact, much of the time, the correct answer to a Weaken question, especially a more difficult one, will not provide an earth-shattering attack. Thus, we need to be adept at finding subtle ways to weaken an argument. 💡MUST KNOW: Often, the correct answer to a Weaken the Argument question will not totally destroy the argument but instead weaken it in a subtle yet important way.
