7.4 FANBOYS Always Alert Us to Check for Proper Parallelism

Lesson Content We already know that the coordinating conjunctions are “for,” “and,” “nor,” “but,” “or,” “yet,” and “so.” We use the mnemonic device FANBOYS to remember them. 💡MUST KNOW: The coordinating conjunctions (FANBOYS): For, And, Nor, But, Or, Yet, So. These FANBOYS conjunctions are always parallelism markers, and a structure built around one of the FANBOYS must be parallel. In other words, any time we encounter one of these FANBOYS conjunctions, we must be vigilant in ensuring that parallelism is properly maintained in the sentence in which it’s found.
