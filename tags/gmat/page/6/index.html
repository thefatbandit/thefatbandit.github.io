3.22 Subject-Verb Agreement Involving the Word “Percentage”

Lesson Content Like “number,” “percentage,” in a sentence subject, can be preceded by either “the” or “a.” When, in a subject, “percentage” is preceded by “the,” the sentence is about the percentage itself, and so the subject is singular, as are the subjects in the following examples.
