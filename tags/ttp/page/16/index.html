7.11 Gerunds and Infinitives – Special Cases

However, despite this similarity between gerunds and infinitives, the convention followed by the GMAT is that gerunds and infinitives are not parallel. 💡MUST KNOW: The GMAT prefers versions that do not use gerunds and infinitives together in lists.
